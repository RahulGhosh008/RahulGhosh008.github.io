Welcome To Bombay Palace

Best Indian Restaurant in Earlsdon, Coventry Since 1984

Bombay Palace has won the Euro Asia BEST RESTAURANT Award 2016 and the Midlands Enterprise Award 2023. Located at the heart of Earlsdon, Coventry, Bombay Palace is an outstanding Indian restaurant in every aspect; the food is divine, the service is incomparable and the ambience inside the restaurant epitomises the classic Earlsdon Street atmosphere - full of vigour and jubilance. Bombay Palace is the perfect restaurant for world class Indian cuisine in Coventry.

Our specialities have been highly recommended by Cobra Good Food Curry Guide and TripAdvisor. Winning top hygiene marks along with top awards, Bombay Palace always welcomes you for a relaxing meal with friendly staff and superior service. We are proud to have served Coventry and the wider West Midlands for over 40 years, bringing authentic flavours of India to Earlsdon Street.
